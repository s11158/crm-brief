The method

What RF microneedling is and why it works

RF microneedling (radiofrequency microneedling) combines two techniques in one treatment. Ultra-fine needles create micro-channels in the skin and, at the same time, deliver radiofrequency (RF) energy into the dermis. Controlled heating triggers the skin's natural repair: new type I collagen, remodeling of collagen fibres and visible firming.

Unlike plain mechanical microneedling, which only works in the superficial layers, RF energy heats the dermis at a set depth — exactly where the skin's support structure lives. That's why patients in Dubai choose RF lifting when they want more than a quick refresh: real density and lift, without surgery.

1

RF energy reaches the dermis and stimulates fibroblasts — the cells that build collagen and elastin.

3

Your own collagen

Your body does the work: the skin keeps firming for weeks after the session.

Technology

Why Virtue RF

Dubai has many RF platforms — Morpheus 8, Genius, Secret and others. We work on Virtue RF, and here's what sets it apart for delicate facial work.

Insulated needles protect the epidermis

The Smart RF tip carries 36 insulated, 24K gold-plated microneedles. Energy is released deeper in the dermis while the skin surface stays protected. This lowers the risk of burns and pigmentation and keeps results predictable on any skin tone.

Micro-pulsed (gated) RF delivery

Virtue RF delivers energy in short, controlled pulses with adjustable pulse width and cycles. Research shows this mode heats the dermis more evenly and deeper with less surface damage — which means more comfort.

Precise depth for every zone

Adjustable needle depth up to 3.5 mm and power let the doctor work gently around the eyes, firmly along the jawline, and precisely on scars and pores. One platform covers face, neck and body.

Comfort and minimal downtime

Fine needles and topical numbing keep the treatment comfortable, while insulation and gated delivery shorten recovery to 1–2 days of light redness.

How collagen is built

In an in-vivo study, bipolar RF treatment with insulated microneedles produced controlled thermal zones in the dermis, and at 14 and 21 days histology showed a marked increase in collagen fibres and fibroblasts — real neocollagenesis, not temporary swelling.

Why it hurts less

Thermometry showed that gated delivery — several short pulses with pauses — spreads energy deeper and more evenly with less surface coagulation. That allows a shallower needle depth and reduces discomfort.

FAQ

Honest answers

How is RF microneedling different from regular microneedling?

Regular microneedling creates only mechanical micro-channels in the superficial layers. Virtue RF additionally delivers radiofrequency energy through the needles and heats the dermis — where the collagen framework is built. That's why the RF version gives visible lifting and firming, not just better texture.

Is RF lifting safe for darker and tanned skin?

Yes. RF energy is colour-blind — it isn't absorbed by melanin, so Virtue RF is safe for every skin tone and all year round, unlike many lasers. Insulated needles further protect the skin surface.

Does it hurt?

The treatment is done under topical numbing and is well tolerated. Fine needles and Virtue RF's gentle gated delivery make it more comfortable than many devices with non-insulated needles.

How many sessions are needed?

Usually a course of 3–4 sessions, 4–6 weeks apart. The doctor confirms the exact number at consultation, depending on the goal — lifting, pores, scars or stretch marks.

What's the downtime?

Minimal. Light redness and a mild sunburn-like sensation last from a few hours to 1–2 days. You can usually apply makeup the next day.

Why Virtue RF over Morpheus 8 or Genius?

All three are quality fractional RF platforms. We choose Virtue RF for its insulated gold-plated needles and gentle gated energy delivery: this is kinder to thin facial skin and the eye area. The right device is always selected for the specific goal.
